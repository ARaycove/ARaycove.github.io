About me

My name is Aaron Raycove, I am currently a university student and I own a variety of companies and spend my time learning and developing myself and working to help others in the process.

I am From

I am from Legos and computer screens

I am from trailer parks, noisy train tracks, and stolen bicycles.

I am from weeds in the curbs and sidewalks, and colorful tulips in mulched beds.

not from lengthy family traditions, but from hard work and dedication. from a vying for a better life.

From a good dad, and a loving grandmother.

I'm from struggle and hardship.

From lots of encouragement, everything from "you're so smart" to "you'll go far" and "I believe in you". Though I never really believed any of it.

I'm from broken homes. I've learned why I don't remember much of my early childhood, but despite that, My father never stopped from trying his best.

I'm from Canada, and Chicago too. Though I'm told we came from Europe many generations ago.

I'm from Mac & Cheese, with hotdogs. . .

From fear and adventure.

From love and heartbreak turned bad-blood, two families "under a truce".

My father started by looking for a better life, and ended up spending it doing his best to make ours better instead. Sacrificing his own ambition in the process.

I think of tragedy, and redemption.

No matter how hard it got they've kept going.

The lives they lived were imperfect, and those who came before made great mistakes and endured much. but that leads us to today, and the story goes on.

I have no mementos or family pictures, no archives to peruse.

Only the memories I carry with me, and I can truly say I'm proud of where I've come from.

and I hope one day my children will be able to say the same.
