About me

My name is Aaron Raycove, I am currently a university student and I own a variety of companies and spend my time learning and developing myself and working to help others in the process.

I am From

I am from Legos and computer screens

I am from trailer parks, noisy train tracks, and stolen bicycles.

I am from weeds in the curbs and sidewalks, and colorful tulips in mulched beds.

not from lengthy family traditions, but from hard work and dedication. from a vying for a better life.

From a good dad, and a loving grandmother.

I'm from struggle and hardship.

From lots of encouragement, everything from "you're so smart" to "you'll go far" and "I believe in you". Though I never really believed any of it.

I'm from broken homes. I've learned why I don't remember much of my early childhood, but despite that, My father never stopped from trying his best.

I'm from Canada, and Chicago too. Though I'm told we came from Europe many generations ago.

I'm from Mac & Cheese, with hotdogs. . .

From fear and adventure.

From love and heartbreak turned bad-blood, two families "under a truce".

My father started by looking for a better life, and ended up spending it doing his best to make ours better instead. Sacrificing his own ambition in the process.

I think of tragedy, and redemption.

No matter how hard it got they've kept going.

The lives they lived were imperfect, and those who came before made great mistakes and endured much. but that leads us to today, and the story goes on.

I have no mementos or family pictures, no archives to peruse.

Only the memories I carry with me, and I can truly say I'm proud of where I've come from.

and I hope one day my children will be able to say the same.

The Worst Day of Your Life

You enter the world under a ring of bright lights, covered in goop, you have to clear your throat. You let out a cry, you can't see, and then you feel a warm embrace.

It's the best, and worst day of your life.

The year passes, and you take your first step. You fall squarely on your bum, a jolt of pain passes and you cry for a long time.

and its the worst day of your life.

You learn to walk and soon to eat, your favorite chocolate milk in hand and curled in your favorite blanket. You misjudge and now a brown stain appears, the milk is gone.

and its the worst day your life.

You learn to run, and soon you fall. A bloody spot on the pavement, and the skin missing from your knee, your father tends to you and it hurts so very much.

It's the worst day of your life

You go to school, and make great friends. Then you have a fight and you aren't friends anymore. You cry, you go home and curl up in your father's arms.

It's the worst day of your life

The years pass, and your first real exams come about. You're confident you think, but you get it back and a failing grade is all you see.

It's the worst day of your life

But school soon ends, a diploma in hand, and a pretty girl around your waist. You wrecked your first car on your way home to see her,

and it's the worst day of your life

Before you know it, kids play happily in the lawn and many great years are behind you, but she says she's not happy anymore and soon she's no more,

and it's the worst day of your life

Now in the middle of your workday, you get a call, it's from the daycare. Your son is on the way to the hospital, they're trying to revive him. But the effort is in vain. You lay your son in a body bag, kiss his forehead one last time,

and it's the worst day of your life

But life goes on, and now you have another baby, she's three. You sit her down for dinner, wrap her in her favorite blanket and give her a glass of chocolate milk, the biggest grin you'll ever see. But she slips and a brown stain appears, her chocolate milk is gone. So you comfort her because

It's the worst day of her life.
